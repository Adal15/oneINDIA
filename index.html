Oriental College Of Education

A powerhouse of cutting-edge education, research & teaching in The World

Career opportunities

  • Mandatory qualification: In many places, the B.Ed. is a mandatory qualification to become a teacher in secondary and senior secondary schools.
  • Diverse roles: Graduates can work as teachers in public or private schools, or pursue roles in educational administration, counseling, curriculum development, and the EdTech industry.

Program structure

  • Duration: The B.Ed. is typically a two-year program, often divided into four semesters.
  • Objective: The main goal is to equip students with the skills and knowledge to become competent and effective educators.
  • Assessment: Coursework includes various assessments and evaluation methods to gauge student learning and progress.
